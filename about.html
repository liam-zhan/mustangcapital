Our Mission

Our mission is to produce superior risk-adjusted returns while exposing Western/Ivey students to a variety of investment and trading strategies across several asset classes. We aim to empower students with practical financial experience, fostering the next generation of investment leaders.

About Mustang Capital

Mustang Capital (formerly Algorithm Trading Club) is a private student interest group that manages a $40,000 investment fund. The fund focuses on producing superior risk-adjusted returns and provides students exposure to a wide range of investment and trading strategies. The club is organized into Investment Verticals and Academy. Investment Verticals focus on specific strategies such as Derivatives, Event-Driven, Global Macro, Quantitative, and Swing Trading, led by experienced professionals. Academy is a training program for first-year students, giving them foundational tools and exposure to all verticals.

Functional Groups

Academy

Academy is an intensive learning program specifically designed for first-year students. Analysts are exposed to all investment verticals and receive a foundational education in markets.

Team Leads: David Kang (PJT Partners), Isaac Dong (Whitehorse Liquidity Partners)

Arbitrage

Focuses on exploiting mispricings in markets, including risk arbitrage, fixed-income arbitrage, and convertible bond arbitrage. Analysts deliver weekly trade pitches.

Team Lead: Michael D'Aiello (TD)

Event Driven

Specializes in analyzing equities influenced by events like M&A, restructuring, and credit ratings. Analysts propose investments based on high-probability outcomes.

Team Leads: Benjamin Chen (Beacon M&A), Nick Oon (Point72)

Global Macro

Analyzes macroeconomic trends across commodities, currencies, and yields to identify investment opportunities in ETFs and country-specific equities.

Team Leads: Jackson Densham (Herculean Group), Judy Zhu (Morgan Stanley, Mawer)

Quantitative Trading

Develops algorithmic trading strategies for markets like crypto and sports betting. Focuses on building software tools and dashboards for risk assessment.

Team Lead: Tristan Jin (Qualcomm, Espresso Capital)

Swing Trading

Combines technical and fundamental analysis to capitalize on medium-term price movements. Analysts monitor charts and identify swing opportunities across asset classes.
